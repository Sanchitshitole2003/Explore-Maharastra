Welcome to the Orange city- Nagpur

Nagpur: The vibrant "Orange City" in central India, blending history, culture, and modernity.

Nagpur has a long history and cultural heritage, with influences from various dynasties and empires. The city is home to several historical landmarks and architectural marvels, including the majestic Nagpur Fort, which was built by the Bhonsle dynasty.

Shri Swaminarayan Temple

A magnificent Hindu temple known for its grand architecture and spiritual significance.



Shri Ganesh Temple Tekdi

A revered Hindu temple nestled on a hill, offering breathtaking views and a serene atmosphere for devotees seeking blessings from Lord Ganesh.


Deekshabhoomi

A revered Buddhist pilgrimage site where Dr. B.R. Ambedkar embraced Buddhism, inspiring spiritual transformation and social equality.


Futala Lake

A captivating spectacle where synchronized fountains and colorful lights create a mesmerizing display over the tranquil waters.


Raman Science Centre

An interactive and educational institution that sparks curiosity and promotes scientific learning through engaging exhibits and activities.


Sitabuldi Fort

Has a storied history, serving as a strategic military stronghold during colonial times and witnessing significant battles during the British Raj.
