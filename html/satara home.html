Welcome to the Rajdhani-Satara

Satara served as one of the principal centers of the Indian Independence Movement. The district of Satara is lined with amazing temples and forts..

"Satara is located in the western part of Maharashtra.Satara is surrounded by hills and forests,Satara is known for its historical monuments and natural beauty "

1.Kas Pathar/Kaas Plateau

Kaas Plateau is a beautiful plateau located in the Western Ghats. It is known for its lush green hills, valleys, and waterfalls. The plateau is also home to a variety of wildlife, including tigers, leopards, and elephants..

2.Pratapgad Fort

Pratapgad Fort is a 17th century fort located in Satara. The fort is known for its strategic location and its role in the Battle of Pratapgad, in which Shivaji Maharaj, the founder of the Maratha Empire, was killed.

know more
3.Thoseghar Falls

Thoseghar Falls is a series of 104 waterfalls located in the Western Ghats. The falls are spread over a distance of 1.5 kilometers and are a popular tourist destination.It is one of the top waterfalls in Maharashtra .

know more
4.Mahabaleshwar

Mahabaleshwar is a scenic hill town in Satara district of Maharashtra. At an altitude of 1,353 m in the Western Ghats, it is one of the popular hill stations in India, and among the top places to visit in Maharashtra.

know more
5.Ajinkyatara Fort

Ajinkyatara is an ancient hill fort situated on one of the seven mountains surrounding the city of Satara in the Sahyadri Mountains of Maharashtra. It is one of the major heritage places in Maharashtra, and among the must include places in Satara tour packages..

know more
6.Shivsagar Lake

Shivsagar Lake, being one of the most beautiful regions in the Satara district offers a peaceful and calm spot to spend an entire day. You can even choose these lakesides as your one-day picnic spot. Cool water here, is quite clean and fresh.
